About Me

Hi there! I'm a dad, a graduate student in plasma physics, and generally a fan of learning something new! If you're reading this, I'm probably somewhere between chasing around a toddler and writing my dissertation, so it's likely the website is still lacking that "polished" feel.

Broadly speaking, my work investigates energy transfer in toroidally-confined plasmas. More explicitly, my research characterizes nonlinear interactions between magnetic field instabilities and populations of energetic ions in tokamaks and/or stellarators. Recently, I have applied wavelet-based bispectral analysis to document wave-wave coupling on sub-millisecond time scales, which may enhance active feedback controls of potentially catastrophic disruptions in forthcoming fusion reactors.

With any luck, I'll be able to post/link/upload enough of my work so that this can function as a back-up of sorts... How does it seem like I'm doing?
